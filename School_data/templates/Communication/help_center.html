Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my portal password?

Please contact the school IT administrator or the Headteacher's office for a password reset request.

Can I view my child's grades from home?

Yes, the Parent Portal allows real-time access to student performance, attendance, and fee status.

What happens if I miss a fee payment?

The Finance office handles all billing issues. You can check your pending invoices in the portal and visit the Accountant for payment plans.
